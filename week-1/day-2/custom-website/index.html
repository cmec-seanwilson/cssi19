My Name is Sean

I'm attending the Google CSSI Camp at Medgar Evers College. I was able to attend this opportunity because Google graciously decided to offer this program to incoming college students. In addition, as a participant in this program, depending on my performance, I may be able to get an internship to Google.

What are we learning?

Google's CSSI Camp is intended to expose us to Computer Science and Web Development. It teaches us the core of how websites work using languages such as HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Moreover, the course also goes in depth into how to navigate Linux's filesystem and how to properly use UNIX.

Who's teaching?

We're being taught by a wonderful set of people. They are all experienced in their craft and some are even part of Medgar Evers.
